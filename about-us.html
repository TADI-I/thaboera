Our Story Our Journey

Our Story

Founded in 2006, Thaboera IT Solutions emerged with a vision to revolutionize the digital landscape by offering innovative IT solutions that drive business growth and technological advancement.

Over the years, Thaboera has evolved, adapting to technological advancements and market demands. Our journey is marked by milestones of successful partnerships, technological innovations, and the trust of our valued clients.

Our client base spans across industries, ranging from startups to established enterprises, each benefiting from our personalized approach and commitment to delivering results.

Our Journey

2006

Company Founded

Thaboera IT Solutions was established with a small team of passionate IT professionals.

2010

First Major Client

Secured our first enterprise client, marking our entry into corporate IT solutions.

2014

Expanded Services

Launched our cloud computing division to meet growing market demands.

2018

New Headquarters

Moved to our current office in Midrand to accommodate our growing team.

2022

International Recognition

Awarded "Best IT Solutions Provider" at the African Tech Awards.
